About Us

MauritiusWonders offers you the widest range of ideas for your trip - lodgings, activities..at the best possible conditions and rates.

Our tour operator was founded nearly 15 years ago. Nowadays, our team welcome more than half of visitors every year and book a quarter of the stays occuring in the island. We give out the best rates and booking conditions.

Thanks to our annual sales volume, we frequently extend unique benefits to our customers, including special excursions, activities, and supplementary amenities. We won't hesistate to recommend changes in your itinerary in order to match your budget more accurately.

Our operational teams (chauffers, guides, hostesses) are committed to you at every step of your trip. From the traditional Mauritian greeting upon arrival at the airport and upon departure, they will ensure that your stay remains perfect.

We ensure that all our products meet the relevant safety and security standards. Our service providers' insurance certificates are scrupulously checked every year.

We are uniquely places to allow the success of your travel project at the best price while meeting safety and high standards.
