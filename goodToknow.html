Good To Know

Tips for a Great Experience

  • Tip 1: The weather varies throughout the year. In summer (November to April), temperatures range from 28 to 32 degrees Celsius, so using sunscreen is advised even on cloudy days. During winter (May to October), temperatures are between 20 and 26 degrees Celsius, and it's recommended to have slightly warmer clothing for the evenings.
  • Tip 2:Do not afraid asking a Mauritian person a question, most know some English and French and can help with directions.
  • Tip 3: It's much hotter near the beach, and cooler near the centre

Emergency Contact

Police: 999

Ambulance: 114

Fire services: 115

Shopping Malls

Stores usually operate between 10:00 AM and 5:00 PM from Monday to Saturday, while certain well-liked malls extend their hours: from 10:00 AM to 7:00 PM on weekdays, until 8:00 PM on weekends, and until 4:00 PM on Sundays and public holidays. Noteworthy shopping destinations include Port Louis' Le Caudan Waterfront, West's Cascavelle Shopping Mall, Ruisseau Creole Centre, Bagatelle, Trianon Shopping Center, Centre Commercial Phoenix North, and Grand Baie La Croisette in the North, and Flacq Coeur de Ville in the East.
